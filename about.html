About PrintMoneyLab Tools

PrintMoneyLab Tools is a collection of free online calculators, comparators, and simulators built to help people make smarter everyday decisions — whether that's comparing AI API costs, figuring out how much it really costs to own a dog, or knowing exactly how many cans of paint your living room needs.

Every tool here is completely free, requires no signup, and works right in your browser. No data collection, no email walls, no premium tiers hiding the useful features.

Why We Built This

Too often, the answer to a simple question is buried in a 2,000-word blog post when all you really need is a quick, interactive calculator. We're filling that gap — one tool at a time.

We focus on niches where search demand is high but good interactive tools are surprisingly hard to find. If you've ever Googled something like "how much paint do I need" and ended up reading five articles instead of just getting the number, you know what we mean.

Who's Behind This

PrintMoneyLab is a solo project documenting the journey of building automated income streams with technology. The tools site is one part of that journey — alongside projects like the blog, crypto trading bots, and various mini-apps.

All tools are built with care, tested for accuracy, and designed to be genuinely useful. If something seems off, let us know.
